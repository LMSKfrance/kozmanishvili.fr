# LinkedIn Strategy — Senior Product Designer
### Optimized for Sanofi & similar enterprise B2B SaaS roles

---

## 🎯 The Strategic Principle

A LinkedIn recruiter scans for **30 seconds**. In those 30 seconds, every signal should say:

> *"Senior Product Designer for B2B SaaS / design systems / healthcare."*

DJ work, brand work, and restaurant client work are real and valuable — but on LinkedIn for this phase, they **dilute the signal** that gets you forwarded.

**Rule:** Don't lie. But don't dilute.

---

**Weak:**
> *"Designer based in Lyon | DJ | Creative Director"*

**Strong (use this):**

**Why:** Recruiters search exact terms. "Senior Product Designer" + "Design Systems" + "B2B" are search keywords. All must be present.

## ❌ HIDE / MINIMIZE

### 1. DJ / Music Work — The Big Decision

**Honest truth:** For pharma/enterprise roles like Sanofi, the DJ identity can read as "split focus" or "not serious about this career path."

**3 options, ranked:**

#### Option A (Safest for Sanofi): Hide entirely from public profile
- Remove DJ/music from Experience
- Keep music passion elsewhere (Instagram, SoundCloud)
- LinkedIn stays 100% product design focused

#### Option B (Balanced): Move to one-line mention
- One line: *"Outside work: techno producer, host on LYL Radio (Lyon)."*
- Doesn't compete with the senior PD narrative
- Shows you're a human

#### Option C (Authentic): Keep but reframe professionally
- Title: *"Cultural Producer / Radio Host @ LYL Radio"* (NOT "DJ")
- Description: Focus on operational side (booking, comms, art direction, streaming, biennale art direction)
- Frames it as event/community work, not nightlife

**Recommendation for next 30 days: Option A.**  
After you land Sanofi (or similar), you can quietly add it back.
