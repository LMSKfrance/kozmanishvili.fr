Methodology

Systems Thinking

I treat UI not as a collection of pages but as a system of reusable logic. Every component is documented, version-controlled, and accessibility-audited before reaching production.

Evidence-Based Validation

Design is a hypothesis. I use contextual inquiry and iterative usability testing to validate decisions with real users before scaling — ensuring operational efficiency matches the clinical and business reality.

Delivery Focus

Design only succeeds if it ships. I specialize in the handoff phase — building documentation and token-linked components that let engineering build with velocity and confidence. Handoff time cut in half. Production bugs down 60%.
